# Settings & Personalization

StudyBible is designed to fit the way *you* read. Open **Settings** from the hamburger menu (App Drawer) to tailor the look, the reading experience, and what appears where.

## Appearance
- **Theme Mode:** Light, Dark, or follow your system setting.
- **Color Scheme:** Choose from curated palettes—**Default Purple**, **Soft Indiglow**, **Modern Indigo**, **Quiet Sage**, **Onyx**, and **Ocean**—or pick **Custom** to build your own.
- **Custom theme:** When you select Custom, you can set your own accent, surface, and text colors independently for light and dark mode, including the color used for the **words of Christ**.
- **Font Family:** Pick the typeface used throughout the app.
- **Text Size & Verse Spacing:** Scale the reading text up or down and adjust the space between verses for comfortable reading. These apply everywhere, so the app stays readable at any size.

## Reading
- **Show Strong's Numbers:** Display clickable Strong's numbers inline when you're using a Strong's-enabled Bible.
- **Reading Progress:** Choose whether chapters are marked read **manually** (tap a button) or **automatically** once you've spent a little time on the page. You can also have a chapter count as read when you finish its audio.
- **Subheadings Source:** Select which installed module provides the inline section headings in the text.
- **Week starts on:** Set the first day of the week for the calendar and streak tracking.

## Dashboard
Toggle each Home screen section—Quick Stats, Verse of the Day, Reading Progress, Reading Plans, Action Items, Time Analytics, and Achievements—on or off. See the **Home & Dashboard** topic for what each one does.

## Verse Sharing
Control how copied and shared verses are formatted:
- **Include verse numbers** — prefix each verse with its number.
- **Include version abbreviation** — cite the source, e.g. *John 3:16 (ESV)*.

A live preview shows exactly how your shared text will look.
