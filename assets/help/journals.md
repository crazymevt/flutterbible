# Journals & Prayers

The Journals tab lets you track your daily walk with God, write your personal thoughts, and manage your prayer list.

## Journals
- **Daily Entries:** Tap a date on the calendar to write a journal entry for that day.
- **Distraction-free Writing:** A clean, full-height editor for capturing your thoughts. Entries save automatically as you write.
- **Revision History:** Every entry keeps past versions—tap the history icon to look back at or restore an earlier draft.
- **Tagging:** Use tags to categorize your journals (e.g. `#gratitude`, `#struggles`). You can search for tags using the global search.

> 💡 **Tip:** To quote Scripture in a journal, copy a verse from the Reader (select it and tap **Copy**) and paste it into your entry. See **Notes, Highlights & Sharing**.

## Prayer Tracker
- Add prayer requests to keep track of what you are praying for.
- Keep notes on how a prayer request evolves over time.
- Mark prayers as "Answered" to celebrate God's faithfulness, moving them to the Answered Prayers list!
