# Sermons

The Sermons tool allows you to take structured notes during church services or personal study, and attach them directly to specific Bible verses!

## Taking Notes
1. Open the Sermons panel from the Study Tools menu.
2. Tap the `+` button to create a new sermon note.
3. Give it a title (e.g., "Sunday Morning: The Beatitudes").
4. Write your notes using the rich text editor.

## Attaching Verses
You can link a sermon note to specific verses. This means whenever you read those verses in the Bible Reader in the future, your sermon notes will appear in the margin or in the Sermons panel!
- In the Sermons panel, tap the "Link Verse" button to attach the current chapter/verse.
- You can also type verse references directly into the note body, and they will become clickable links!
