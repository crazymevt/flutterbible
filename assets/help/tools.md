# Study Tools

The side panel (or the bottom sheet on mobile) contains a powerful suite of tools to help you study the Scriptures. You can switch between tools using the navigation rail.

## Compare
Select a single verse in the Reader to view how it is translated across *every single Bible* you have downloaded, stacked vertically.

## Cross-References
View a list of related verses for the chapter you are currently reading. Tapping a cross-reference will open it in a preview window, allowing you to read it in context without losing your place in the main Reader.

## Commentaries
Read verse-by-verse commentary from classic theologians like Matthew Henry or John Wesley. You must first download commentary modules from the Content Manager.

## Dictionaries
Search for specific words or topics across all your downloaded dictionaries. This is great for doing word studies or looking up cultural context.

## Reading Plans
Track your progress through various reading plans. 

## Devotionals
Read daily devotionals (like Spurgeon's *Morning and Evening*).

## Topics & Places
Explore Biblical topics and geographical locations referenced in the text.
